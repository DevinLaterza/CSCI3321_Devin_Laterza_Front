Smokehouse BBQ Burger

Ingredients

  • 1lb hamburger meat
  • 1lb bacon
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 onion

Directions

  1. Slice onion and sautee until edges are crispy
  2. Fry bacon until partially crispy
  3. Grill hamburger patties 8 min. on each side until fully cooked
  4. Apply BBQ sauce to buns, assemble and serve!












Chicken Fried Steak

Ingredients

  • Four cube steaks
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • salt and black pepper
  • 2.5 cups of whole milk
  • 4.5 cups vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Tenderize cube steaks
  2. Begin making gravy by whisking 3 tbsp. flour with milk on med. high heat
  3. Add 1 tsp. salt and 1 tsp. pepper
  4. Whisk until gravy thickens then remove from heat
  5. Dip tenderized steaks in eggs and cover with flower
  6. Fry in vegetable oil on med. high heat until golden brown
  7. Serve with gravy on top
